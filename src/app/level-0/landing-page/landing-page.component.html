koda

A self-learning coding platform for kids.

It's a fun journey which improves their logical skills and enhances their creative thinking skills.

It is a well-known fact that the education of yesterday won't help our children solve problems of tomorrow. Therefore, leading educators have emphasized the importance of imbibing 21st Century Skills (Creativity, Critical Thinking, Collaboration and Communication) in our education. Koda is a block-based coding platform which explicitly focuses on developing these skills in a child. Designing their own games also help in building the computational thinking skills of children, which prepares them to solve complex problems across other domains/subjects as well.
